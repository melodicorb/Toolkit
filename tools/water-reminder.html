Hydration Tips

Why Stay Hydrated?

Proper hydration improves concentration, memory, and cognitive performance - essential for effective studying.

How Much Water?

The general recommendation is about 2 liters (8 cups) of water per day, but needs vary based on activity level and environment.

Signs of Dehydration

Watch for headaches, fatigue, dry mouth, and difficulty concentrating - all can be signs you need more water.

Hydration Habits

Keep a water bottle at your study space, drink a glass of water between study sessions, and start your day with water.
