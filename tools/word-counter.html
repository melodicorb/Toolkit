Word Counter

Count words, characters, sentences and paragraphs in your text

About This Tool

The Word Counter tool helps you analyze your text by counting words, characters, sentences, and paragraphs. It's perfect for students working on essays with specific length requirements, writers tracking their progress, or anyone who needs to ensure their text meets certain criteria. The tool also provides detailed statistics like reading time, speaking time, and word frequency analysis.

How to Use

  1. Type or paste your text in the input box
  2. View the real-time statistics as you type
  3. Check detailed statistics like reading time and word frequency
  4. Use the clear button to start over or the copy button to copy your text
  5. Adjust the minimum word length slider to filter word frequency results

Frequently Asked Questions

How is reading time calculated?

Reading time is calculated based on an average reading speed of 225 words per minute, which is the typical reading speed for most adults.

How is speaking time calculated?

Speaking time is calculated based on an average speaking rate of 150 words per minute, which is the typical pace for presentations or speeches.

What counts as a word?

Any sequence of characters separated by spaces or punctuation is counted as a word. Numbers and special characters may be counted as words if they're separated by spaces.

Does the tool save my text?

No, your text is processed entirely in your browser and is not saved or transmitted to any server. When you close or refresh the page, your text will be lost.
