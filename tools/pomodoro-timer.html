Pomodoro Timer

Stay focused with timed work sessions and breaks

About This Tool

The Pomodoro Technique is a time management method that uses a timer to break work into intervals, traditionally 25 minutes in length, separated by short breaks. This technique helps improve focus and productivity by creating a sense of urgency and providing regular mental refreshment.

How to Use

  1. Set your desired work time, break time, and long break time
  2. Click the Start button to begin your work session
  3. Focus on your task until the timer ends
  4. Take a short break when prompted
  5. After completing a set number of work sessions, enjoy a longer break

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Pomodoro Technique?

The Pomodoro Technique is a time management method developed by Francesco Cirillo in the late 1980s. It uses a timer to break work into intervals, traditionally 25 minutes in length, separated by short breaks.

Why should I use the Pomodoro Technique?

This technique helps combat procrastination, improves focus and concentration, reduces mental fatigue, and increases accountability and motivation.

Can I customize the timer intervals?

Yes! You can adjust the work time, break time, and long break time to suit your personal preferences and workflow.

Does the timer save my progress?

The timer tracks your completed sessions in the Session History section, but this data is not permanently saved between browser sessions.
