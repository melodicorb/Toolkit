About This Tool

The Citation Generator helps you create accurate citations for your research papers, essays, and other academic work. This tool supports multiple citation styles including APA, MLA, Chicago, and Harvard, ensuring your references are properly formatted according to academic standards.

How to Use

  1. Select your preferred citation style (APA, MLA, Chicago, or Harvard)
  2. Choose the source type (book, journal article, website, etc.)
  3. Fill in the required information for your source
  4. Click "Generate Citation" to create your formatted citation
  5. Copy the citation to your clipboard or add it to your bibliography

Frequently Asked Questions

Which citation styles are supported?

Currently, we support APA (7th Edition), MLA (9th Edition), Chicago (17th Edition), and Harvard (2021 Edition) citation styles.

What source types can I cite?

You can create citations for books, journal articles, websites, and more. We're continuously adding support for additional source types.

Are the citations stored anywhere?

Citations are stored locally in your browser's storage. They are not sent to any server and will be available only on your current device.

Can I export my bibliography?

Yes, you can copy individual citations or export your entire bibliography as a text file for use in your documents.

Citation Tips

Accuracy Matters

Always double-check your citation information for accuracy. Small errors can make it difficult for readers to locate your sources.

Consistency is Key

Use the same citation style throughout your entire paper. Mixing styles can confuse readers and affect your credibility.

Know Your Style Guide

Familiarize yourself with the specific requirements of your chosen citation style. Each style has unique formatting rules.
