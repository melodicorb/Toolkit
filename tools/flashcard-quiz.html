Flashcard Quiz

Create and take quizzes to test your knowledge

About This Tool

The Flashcard Quiz tool allows you to create, manage, and take interactive quizzes with multiple-choice questions. Perfect for self-assessment, exam preparation, or knowledge retention, this tool helps you test your understanding of any subject through customizable quiz decks that you can revisit anytime.

How to Use

  1. Click "Create New Quiz" to start a new quiz deck
  2. Enter a name, description, and category for your quiz
  3. Add multiple-choice questions with correct and incorrect answer options
  4. Click "Start Quiz" when you're ready to test your knowledge
  5. Answer each question and check your responses
  6. Review your final score and performance at the end of the quiz

Frequently Asked Questions

How many quizzes can I create?

You can create as many quiz decks as you need. All quizzes are stored locally in your browser's storage.

Can I edit my quizzes after creating them?

Yes, you can edit quiz details, add new questions, modify existing questions, or delete questions at any time.

Is there a limit to how many questions I can add?

There's no practical limit to the number of questions you can add to a quiz, though very large quizzes may affect performance on some devices.

Can I share my quizzes with others?

Currently, quizzes are stored locally on your device and cannot be directly shared. However, you can export/import functionality may be added in future updates.
