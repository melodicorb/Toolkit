Flashcards

Create and study digital flashcards to improve your memory retention

About This Tool

The Flashcards tool helps you create digital study cards to memorize and review important information. Perfect for learning vocabulary, definitions, formulas, or any content that requires repetition and active recall, this tool makes studying more efficient and effective.

How to Use

  1. Click "Create New Deck" to start a collection of related flashcards
  2. Name your deck, add a description, and select a category
  3. Add cards to your deck with front (question) and back (answer) content
  4. Click "Study Cards" to begin a study session
  5. Use the "Flip Card" button to reveal answers
  6. Navigate through cards with the Previous and Next buttons
  7. Use "Shuffle Cards" for randomized study sessions

Frequently Asked Questions

How many cards can I add to a deck?

There's no specific limit to the number of cards per deck, but keeping decks focused on specific topics with 20-50 cards is often most effective for learning.

Can I include images in my flashcards?

The current version supports text only, but we're considering adding image support in a future update.

Can I export or share my flashcards?

Currently, flashcards are stored locally in your browser and cannot be exported or shared. This feature may be added in future updates.

How does the spaced repetition system work?

The current version doesn't include spaced repetition, but you can manually shuffle and review cards at intervals that work best for your learning style.
