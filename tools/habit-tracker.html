Habit Tracker

Build consistent study habits and track your progress over time

About This Tool

The Habit Tracker is designed to help you build and maintain consistent study habits. Whether you're trying to establish a daily reading routine, regular exercise, or consistent study sessions, this tool will help you stay accountable and visualize your progress over time.

How to Use

  1. Click "Create New Habit" to define a new habit you want to track
  2. Give your habit a name, description, and set its frequency
  3. Choose a color to visually distinguish different habits
  4. Check in daily to mark your habits as complete
  5. View your progress on the monthly calendar
  6. Track your streaks and completion rates in the statistics section

Frequently Asked Questions

How do streaks work?

A streak counts consecutive days where you've completed a habit. Missing a day will reset your current streak, but your longest streak will still be saved.

Can I track multiple habits at once?

Yes! You can create and track as many habits as you need. Each habit will be color-coded for easy identification.

What happens if I miss a day?

Missing a day will affect your streak and completion rate, but you can always get back on track. The tool is designed to help you build consistency over time.

Can I edit or delete a habit?

Yes, you can edit habit details or remove habits that are no longer relevant to your goals.
