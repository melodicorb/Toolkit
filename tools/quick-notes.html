Quick Notes

Capture and organize your thoughts quickly and efficiently

About This Tool

Quick Notes is a versatile note-taking tool designed to help you capture ideas, lecture notes, research findings, and any important information quickly and efficiently. Organize your notes with categories, search through them easily, and access them anytime you need.

How to Use

  1. Click "New Note" to create a new note
  2. Give your note a title and optional category
  3. Write your content in the editor
  4. Notes are automatically saved as you type
  5. Use the search function to find specific notes
  6. Filter notes by category using the sidebar

Frequently Asked Questions

Are my notes saved automatically?

Yes! Your notes are automatically saved to your browser's local storage as you type, so you won't lose your work.

Is there a limit to how many notes I can create?

There's no hard limit, but since notes are stored in your browser's local storage, very large numbers of notes might eventually use up available space.

Can I format my notes with bold, italics, etc.?

The current version supports plain text only, but we're considering adding rich text formatting in a future update.

Can I access my notes from different devices?

Notes are stored locally in your browser, so they're only available on the device where you created them. Consider using the same browser across devices for consistency.
