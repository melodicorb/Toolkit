Bibliography Generator

Create and manage complete bibliographies for your academic papers

About This Tool

The Bibliography Generator is a comprehensive tool designed to help students create properly formatted citations and bibliographies for academic papers. This tool supports multiple citation styles including APA, MLA, Chicago, and Harvard, making it versatile for various academic disciplines.

Key Features

  • Support for multiple citation styles (APA, MLA, Chicago, Harvard)
  • Various source types (books, websites, journals, and more)
  • Save and manage your bibliography entries
  • Export formatted bibliographies for your papers
  • Automatic formatting according to style guidelines

How It Helps

  • Saves time by automating citation formatting
  • Ensures accuracy in your academic references
  • Helps avoid plagiarism by properly crediting sources
  • Organizes all your sources in one place
  • Simplifies the research paper writing process

User Guide

Step 1: Select Citation Style

Choose your required citation style (APA, MLA, Chicago, or Harvard) by clicking the corresponding button at the top of the tool.

Step 2: Choose Source Type

Select the type of source you're citing (book, website, journal article, etc.) to display the appropriate form fields.

Step 3: Enter Source Details

Fill in all required fields (marked with *) and any optional fields that apply to your source. For multiple authors, use the "Add Another Author" button.

Step 4: Generate Citation

Click the "Generate Citation" button to create your formatted citation based on the information provided.

Step 5: Manage Your Bibliography

Save citations to your bibliography list, edit existing entries, or export your complete bibliography for use in your paper.

Frequently Asked Questions

Which citation styles are supported?

Currently, we support APA (7th Edition), MLA (9th Edition), Chicago (17th Edition), and Harvard citation styles. We plan to add more styles in future updates.

Can I save my bibliography for later use?

Yes! Your bibliography is automatically saved in your browser's local storage. However, we recommend exporting important bibliographies as they may be lost if you clear your browser data.

How do I cite sources with multiple authors?

Use the "Add Another Author" button to add fields for each additional author. The citation will be formatted according to the selected style's guidelines for multiple authors.

What if my source type isn't listed?

Click the "More" button under Source Type to see additional source types. If your specific source still isn't listed, choose the closest match and include additional details in notes if needed.

Can I edit citations after creating them?

Yes, you can edit any citation in your saved bibliography by clicking the edit icon next to the entry. This will reload the form with your previous information for modification.
