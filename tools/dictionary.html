About This Tool

The Dictionary tool provides quick access to word definitions, pronunciations, and usage examples. Perfect for students, writers, and language learners, this tool helps you understand unfamiliar words and expand your vocabulary with comprehensive linguistic information.

How to Use

  1. Type a word in the search box
  2. Click the search button or press Enter
  3. View the word's definition, pronunciation, and examples
  4. Listen to the pronunciation by clicking the audio button (when available)
  5. Browse your recent searches for quick access to previously looked-up words

Frequently Asked Questions

Which dictionary source does this tool use?

This tool uses the Free Dictionary API, which provides comprehensive word information from multiple trusted dictionary sources.

Does it work offline?

No, an internet connection is required to look up new words. However, your recent searches are stored locally and can be viewed without an internet connection.

Can I look up words in other languages?

Currently, the dictionary primarily supports English words. Support for additional languages may be added in future updates.

How many recent searches are saved?

The tool saves your 10 most recent searches for quick access. These are stored locally in your browser.
