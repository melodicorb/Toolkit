Privacy Policy

Last Updated:

Introduction

Welcome to Ultimate Student Toolkit. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy will inform you about how we look after your personal data when you visit our website and tell you about your privacy rights and how the law protects you.

Information We Collect

Our toolkit operates entirely within your browser and does not require you to create an account or provide any personal information. We collect the following types of information:

  • Usage data stored in your browser's local storage
  • Anonymous analytics data to improve our services

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our tools
  • Save your preferences and tool data locally on your device
  • Analyze usage patterns to enhance user experience

Local Storage

Our tools use your browser's local storage to save your data directly on your device. This means:

  • Your data remains on your device and is not transmitted to our servers
  • You maintain control over your data at all times
  • You can clear this data at any time by clearing your browser's local storage

Advertisements

Our website displays advertisements to support our free tools. These advertisements may use cookies to serve ads based on your interests. We do not control these third-party cookies. You can opt out of interest-based advertising by visiting:

  • Digital Advertising Alliance
  • European Interactive Digital Advertising Alliance

Children's Privacy

Our tools are designed for general audiences and do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us so we can remove the information.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through our Contact Page.
