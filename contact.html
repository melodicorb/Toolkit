Frequently Asked Questions

Are all the tools really free to use?

Yes, all 30 tools in the Ultimate Student Toolkit are completely free to use. We believe in providing accessible resources for students and professionals.

Do I need to create an account to use the tools?

No account is required. All tools work directly in your browser, and your data is stored locally on your device for privacy.

How can I suggest a new tool or feature?

We welcome your suggestions! Simply use the contact form on this page and select "Feature Request" from the subject dropdown.

Is my data secure when using these tools?

Your privacy is important to us. All data is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers. For more information, please see our Privacy Policy.
